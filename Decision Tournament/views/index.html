Decision Tournament

Enter your list of options and watch them fight to the death to decide the ultimate victor.

Enter each of your options in the boxes provided, one per box.

If you would like to quickly remove an option then press the X button to the right of that text box.

If you would like to add even more options press the + button at the bottom of the options list.

To begin the descision tournament press the 'Fight!' button.
